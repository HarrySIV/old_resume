Why should we hire you over x, y, z person?

Help me help you and we'll both come out on top.

Tell me about a hardship you had and how you overcame it.

Believe me when I say, I've had many a hardships, work related and otherwise. But I suppose I should be specific. At my first Chick-Fil-A, I had moved up from team member to Night Manager. I was in charge of closing, but I also had other responsibilities, including helping roll out CFA's new Drive-Thru order taking system. It was tough to be sure, we had to get new equipment and test out different ways to organize this system, as well as look online to see what other CFA's were doing to improve it. I created excel spreadsheets to track out service times hour by hour and we (as a team) would experiment relentlessly to see what worked and what didn't, as well as where our bottlenecks lied and why. Basically, I tried to bring useful data to the situation and was adaptive and flexible in solving the problem until we had some of the best Drive-Thru times in the company.
